CABBAGE

Rich in Antioxidants:
Cabbage is packed with antioxidants, which fight free radicals and reduce the risk of chronic diseases.
Heart Health:
It helps improve cholesterol levels and supports cardiovascular health.
Skin Glow:
High in vitamin C, cabbage promotes collagen production and protects against skin damage.
Weight-Friendly:
Low in calories and high in fiber, it is perfect for digestion and weight management. Versatile in Cooking:
From stir-fries to salads, cabbage enhances flavor and nutrition in countless dishes.
