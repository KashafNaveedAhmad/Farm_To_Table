PEAS

Rich in Nutrients:
Peas are packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that support overall health and boost immunity.
Heart Health:
Rich in fiber and antioxidants, peas help lower cholesterol and support cardiovascular health.
Skin Benefits:
Peas contain vitamin C, which promotes collagen production and helps protect the skin from damage.
Weight-Friendly:
Low in calories and high in fiber, peas are excellent for digestion and weight management. Versatile in Cooking:
From soups to stews, peas add flavor, texture, and nutrition to various dishes.
