CARROTS



CARROT

Carrot Benefits:
Carrots are rich in vitamin A, which supports healthy vision and immune function.
Heart Health:
Carrots contain fiber and antioxidants, which promote heart health.
Skin Benefits:
Carrots are often used in skincare to rejuvenate and hydrate the skin.
Weight-Friendly:
Carrots are low in calories and high in water, which makes them ideal for weight management.
Versatile in Cooking:
Carrots can be eaten raw, roasted, or added to soups and stews.
