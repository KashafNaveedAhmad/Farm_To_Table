CORN

Rich in Nutrients:
Corn is packed with essential vitamins and minerals, including vitamin C, fiber, and B vitamins that support overall health.
Heart Health:
Corn is a good source of fiber and antioxidants, which help lower cholesterol and support cardiovascular health.
Eye Health:
Rich in lutein and zeaxanthin, corn promotes eye health by protecting against age-related vision problems.
Energy Boosting:
Corn is a complex carbohydrate that provides a slow and steady energy release, making it ideal for a quick energy boost. Versatile in Cooking:
From salads to soups, corn can be used in a variety of dishes to add a sweet and savory flavor.
