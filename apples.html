APPLES

Rich in Antioxidants:
Apples are rich in antioxidants, including quercetin and polyphenols, which help fight free radicals and reduce the risk of chronic diseases.
Heart Health:
They support cardiovascular health by lowering cholesterol levels and improving blood circulation.
Skin Glow:
High in vitamin C and hydration, apples promote collagen production, detoxify the skin, and support a healthy glow.
Weight-Friendly:
Low in calories and high in fiber, apples are a perfect snack for weight management and digestive health. Versatile in Use:
From fresh slices to pies, juices, and salads, apples add flavor and nutrition to a wide range of dishes.
